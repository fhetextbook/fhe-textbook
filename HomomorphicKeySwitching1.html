D-2.8 Homomorphic Key Switching

BFV’s key switching scheme changes an RLWE ciphertext’s secret key from S to S′. This scheme is essentially RLWE’s key switching scheme with the sign of the A ⋅S term flipped in the encryption and decryption formula. Specifically, this is equivalent to the alternative GLWE version’s (§B-4.4) key switching scheme (§C-5) with k = 1 as follows:
